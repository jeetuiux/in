What we do ?

At The Car Guys Studio, we provide expert car detailing services designed to enhance, protect, and preserve your vehicle. We pride ourselves on delivering unmatched quality, meticulous attention to detail, and long-lasting results you can see and feel. Every car we service is treated with precision craftsmanship, using premium products, modern techniques, and a passion for perfection. With us, it’s not just about a clean car — it’s about bringing out the best version of your vehicle.

Services

Check our Services

Exterior & Interior Car Wash

Thorough cleaning inside and out — from a spotless exterior to a fresh, dust-free interior. We remove dirt, grime, and stains to leave your car looking and feeling brand new.

Vacuuming & Interior Cleaning

We meticulously clean every corner of your car’s interior — removing dust, debris, and hidden dirt with powerful vacuuming and deep cleaning for seats, carpets, mats, and more.

Polish & Paint Protection

We restore your car’s gloss with expert polishing and protect the paint with a durable layer that guards against scratches, UV rays, and environmental damage — keeping your vehicle shining longer.

Ceramic Coating & Graphene Coating

Advanced protection for your car’s paint — ceramic and graphene coatings create a long-lasting, high-gloss shield that repels water, resists scratches, and defends against UV rays, dirt, and chemicals.

Headlight
Restoration

We restore clarity to dull, yellowed, or foggy headlights — improving visibility, safety, and your vehicle’s overall appearance with a clean, like-new finish.

Paint Protection Film
(PPF)

A nearly invisible, high-strength film applied to your car’s surface to protect against scratches, stone chips, and harsh elements — preserving your paint’s flawless finish for years.

Denting & Painting Services

From minor dents to major damage, we restore your car’s body with precision dent repair and high-quality painting — perfectly matched to your original finish for a seamless look.

Premium Car Accessories Installation

Upgrade your ride with high-quality accessories — from stylish interiors to functional add-ons, we ensure expert installation for a perfect fit, clean finish, and enhanced driving experience.
